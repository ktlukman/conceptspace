Photo Shoots

Lights, camera, action! Get ready for your brand close up

Introduction

Brand Imagery is one tool within your Brand Identity artillery that defines the aesthetic appearance of your brand's core messaging. It’s the key visual story-telling component of your brand.

We understand that every project has its own unique set of challenges and requirements, which is why we have ensured that our production services are fully customizable. Whether you work in beauty, fashion, lifestyle, food, tech or something totally different, we have an in-house team of creatives who are eager to manage every aspect, or just partial elements of your content creation project. From art direction and concept development to styling and set design, we create bespoke packages which cater to your brand’s specific strategic and budgetary requirements.

Art Direction..
 Art Direction..
We manage creative photo shoots, acting as the intermediary between your design team and other external resources to drive the artistic brand vision while coordinating with all project stakeholders. We provide expert knowledge of all visual mediums including lighting, camera angles and other creative coordination. Dedicated to bringing your story to life; we work alongside your team from the initial concept development phases through to the final stage of production.

Set Design

We are on constantly on the lookout for emerging trends, styles and materials. Creating sets that are unique and custom made for your shoot to tell your story. Whether you’re shooting videos, editorials or influencer content, we create unique and imaginative setups that resonate with your brand’s audience

Our team’s diverse backgrounds and experiences deliver ideas and concepts that resonate with audiences around the world. We bring decades of storytelling experience to the table. We specialize in creating stand-out content for influencer campaigns, social media, advertising, editorials and television commercials. Whether it’s still images, film, stop motion or some digital magic; we are experts at calibrating each piece of content catering to different media platforms and their respective audiences.
