Our Projects

Storytelling through innovative out of the box design solutions

Introduction

We have accumulated decades of global experience in brand design and development which enables us to build powerful brands that stand out, covert sales and are designed to last. Take a peek at our recent creative projects to visualize how our concepts can drive your next project.

Pan Home – Rebrand Shoot

The key mandate of this campaign was to transform the image of the Pan Emirates legacy brand into a modern brand that is able to stand on its own next to well established global furnishing brands. This 360 overhaul involved a campaign shoot and complete visual and strategic overhaul which included the brand name and logo change to Pan Home.

Readmore...

Pan Home – High-End Shoot

Our objective with this shoot was to elevate Pan Home’s furniture collection and feature it in an elevated luxuries aesthetic. The campaign is product focused with minimal decoration, highlighting product beauty and quality from every angle. Shot in a conceptual style, the campaign looks elevated and luxurious.

Pan Home – Ramadan Shoot

Ramadan is a special time and we created concept with an objective that resonates with the Pan target audience on an emotional level. We built the campaign around family, the diverse heritage and traditions that we see during Ramadan. The result was a beautifully rustic, aesthetic and elevated campaign that connected to the hearts of their audience.

Pan Home – Sleep Campaign Shoot

With the sleep campaign our objective is to encapsulate the relaxation aspect and effortless comfort that comes we associate we rest and sleep. We took extra care in styling the beds, the key aspects of the campaign to make them appear snug, comfy and stylish. The campaign highlights the various sleep solutions with softness and lightness. Playing with soft shadows and light to show the passing of day.

Aldo – Step into Love Shoot

Aldo was looking launch its sustainable collection in the ME and needed a stylish campaign that was aligning with their global brand strategy but at same time also resonated with the local market. With carefully selected talent and locations we were able to successfully launch the new collection, translating the brand ethos while raising awareness of this sustainable collection.

Pan Home – Rebrand Launch Event

As part of the rebrand of Pan Emirates to Pan home, our goal with the rebrand launch event was to feature the new brand and show case the new aesthetic and elevated style. Every detail was very intentional, from activations such as custom engravings, to custom art, from invites to gifting, from setup to location selection, from catering to the grand reveal, every detail was especially curated to create an ambiance of luxury in order to set the right tone for the new brand from the get go.

Pan Home – Studio Photography Overhaul

The home furnishing brand’s ecommerce studio photography needed a major overhaul as photos were looking dull, styling was overdone, and the final edits were looking inconsistent. We worked closely with the Pan studio team on preparing styling guidelines, training VMs, photo editors and photographers as well advising on studio setups and equipment to make noticeable improvements.

Brand Identity Development
